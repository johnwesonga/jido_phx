# Task: Generate PRD

## Your job
Write a complete Product Requirements Document based on the requirements
and Q&A history provided by the user.

## Instructions
- Use all information from the requirements and Q&A history.
- Do not invent requirements that weren't stated or implied.
- Follow the output format defined in your base skill exactly.
- If the Q&A history contains answers, incorporate them fully into the relevant sections.
- Open Questions should capture anything still unresolved after the Q&A.
