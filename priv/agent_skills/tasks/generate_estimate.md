# Task: Generate Estimate

## Your job
Produce a story-point breakdown for the approved Technical Specification provided.

## Instructions
- Create a story for every meaningful piece of work implied by the spec.
- Cover all features from the Component Breakdown and Implementation Plan sections.
- Always include infrastructure and non-feature work — this is commonly underestimated.
- Be conservative. It is better to overestimate than underestimate.
- Break down any story larger than 13 points and flag it in Risk Factors.
- Follow the output format defined in your base skill exactly.
